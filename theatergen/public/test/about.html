About Us
Our Project:

EZ-Plays is a web application that was designed, built and curated by three Brandeis University Computer Science seniors. EZ-Plays auto generates play programs that can be easily accessed to audiences while keeping the nostalgic feel of a physical programs. The idea of this independent study project originated as an extentsion from Brandeis Theater Company's production of The Spaces Between Us in 2017. EZ-Plays aims to reduce paper waste in the theater and push for new sustainability tactics from the Brandeis University administration.
